Our Story

Founded in 1998 by Imad Mahfouz, Vertica has grown from a small boutique in Dbayeh into a renowned destination for bespoke floral arrangements and unforgettable wedding experiences. For over two decades, we've been dedicated to creating beautiful moments with elegance and creativity, earning a reputation as one of the most trusted names in the industry. Join us as we continue to craft stunning floral designs and magical weddings, turning dreams into reality.

Beyond the Veil
Crafting Memorable Moments

At Vertica, we don't just plan weddings; we orchestrate unforgettable experiences. Behind the scenes, our dedicated team ensures every detail is perfect, guided by a commitment to excellence and a passion for creativity.

From the initial consultation to the final farewell, we address our clients with warmth and understanding, making them feel like cherished friends. With innovation at our core, we set new standards in the wedding industry, infusing each event with personal touches that reflect our clients' unique love stories.

Join us as we continue to push boundaries, creating magical memories that last a lifetime.
